ATRIX RTVC 2.0!

RTVC 2.0 ( Real Time Virtual Classroom) enable users to provide interactive audio, video calling or screen sharing experience for customers with excellance..

It uses RTCMulticonnection.js and Signaling.js to handle webRTC signaling. With RTVC you can build any sort of audio calling, video calling, webinar, live hosting or screen sharing web application :)

Real Time Virtual Classroom

RTVC is a powerful tool that serve as seed project for developing 1 to 1 or 1 to many audio video calling over the internet without use of any external API.
Creating your very own video calling web application or integrating RTVC into your project for your users to interact better over video calling or providing your customers to contact you via voice call or video call and let them interact with latest technology in built in your applicaiton.

History

RTVC 1.0 was created in June, 2017 for support of AngularJS 1.5.x and its serve as a first wrapper for videoc calling by providing direct directive and service functions to be used for building webRTC apps using AngularJS however this project totally depends upon RTCMulticonnection v3, special Thanks to Muaz Khan

RTVC 2.0 beta was launched in January 2018 Created by: Moeid Saleem Khan, Atrix Digital Solutions.
